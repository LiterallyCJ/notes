## Properties of complex numbers and their conjugates:

$$
\begin{gather*}
z = z^* \text{ if } z \in \mathbb{R} \\
z \cdot z^* = |z|^2 \\
(z + w)^* = z^* + w^* \\
(zw)^* = z^* \cdot w^*
\end{gather*}
$$

$$
\begin{gather*}
\text{If } z \in \mathbb{C} \text{ and } z \neq 0, \\
z^{-1}  = \frac{1}{|z|^2} \cdot z^*
\end{gather*}
