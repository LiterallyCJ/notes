For any integer $s$, we define $$
\begin{align}
\text{ if } s > 0,\ & A^s = A \cdot A \cdot \underbrace{\dots}_{\text{s times}} \cdot A  \\
\text{ if } s = 0,\ & A^s = I \\
\text{ if } s < 0,\ & A^s = A^{-1} + \underbrace{\dots}_{\text{s times}} \cdot A^{-1}
\end{align}
