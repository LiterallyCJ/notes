## <u>Variance</u>

$$
\frac{1}{n-1}\underbrace{\sum_{i = 1}^{n}(x - \bar x)^2}_{SSE(\bar x)}, \text{ where } \bar x = \frac{1}{n}\sum^{n}_{i = 1}x_i
$$

$$
\begin{align}
\sum_{i = 1}^{n}(x_i - \bar x)^2 &= \sum_{i = 1}^{n}(x_i^2 - 2 \bar x x_i + \bar x^2)
$= \sum_{i = 1}^{n}
\end{align}
$$
