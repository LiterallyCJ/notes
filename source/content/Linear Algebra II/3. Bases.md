
### Spanning

Let $V$ be a vector space over a field $F$. Let $x_1, \dots, x_n \in V$.

1. An element $x \in V$ is called a linear combination of $x_1, \dots, x_n$ if there are $a_1, \dots, a_n$ such that $x = a_1x_1 + \dots + a_nx_n$
2. The subset of $V$ consisting of all linear combinations of $x_1, \dots, x_n$ is called the span of $x_1, \dots, x_n$ and is denoted by $Span(x_1, \dots, x_n)$ or $Span_F(x_1, \dots, x_n)$ i.e
$$
Span(x_1, \dots, x_n) = \{a_1x_1 + \dots + a_nx_n | a_1, \dots, a_n \in F\}
$$
