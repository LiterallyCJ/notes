
### Subspaces

Let $V$ be a vector space over a field $F$. A subset $W$ of $V$ is called a subspace of $V$ if the following conditions are true:

1. $0_v \in W$
2. $W$ is closed under addition; $\forall x, y \in W, x + y \in W$
3. $W$ is closed under scalar multiplication; $\forall a \in F, x \in W, ax \in W$

Note that the second condition states that the restriction of addition in $V$ to $W$ gives a binary operation $W \times W \to W$ on $W$.
